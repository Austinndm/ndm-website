A commercial repair operation built for heavy-duty work

Northern Desert Mechanical exists to serve operators who depend on diesel equipment, working fleets, and mechanically reliable assets to stay productive.

What NDM does

Northern Desert Mechanical focuses on B2B maintenance and repair for commercial and industrial customers. The work ranges from routine preventive maintenance to major mechanical repairs, including engine changes and heavy-duty diagnostics.

Unlike light-duty automotive shops, NDM is oriented around the demands of working equipment. That means understanding downtime pressure, fleet scheduling, industrial jobsite realities, and the need for practical repair decisions.

Its strongest specialty is charter bus repair, supported by broader capability across over-the-road semis, industrial support equipment, diesel-powered site assets, and general equipment repairs.

Why clients work with NDM

Shop and fleet capability

NDM combines a physical shop with mobile service assets sized for different levels of field support.

Dedicated Shop

A service base for inspections, scheduled maintenance, and larger mechanical repairs requiring controlled shop work.

Fully Equipped Service Trucks

Mobile field support for inspections, diagnostics, maintenance, and on-site repair response across a wide range of commercial equipment.

Heavy Field Capability

Expanded support for demanding repair situations that require more tools, parts handling, and dependable on-site service capability.

Crane Truck Support

Added lifting and handling capability for heavier components, more complex field repairs, and jobs that require crane-assisted service.
