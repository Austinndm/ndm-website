Service capability built around commercial uptime

Northern Desert Mechanical supports customers who need more than basic service - from scheduled PMs to engine changes, field response, and equipment-specific repair work.

Preventive Maintenance Programs

Routine maintenance is the backbone of reliable fleet and equipment performance. NDM provides scheduled service support designed to catch issues early and keep assets on predictable intervals.

  • PM inspections and scheduled service intervals
  • Fluids, filters, belts, cooling system checks, and general inspections
  • Maintenance support for fleets, buses, semis, and site equipment
  • Service planning for commercial operations

Mobile HVAC & Climate Control

HVAC systems on buses and commercial vehicles require real diagnostic skill - not just parts swapping. NDM specializes in AC and heating repair for mobile equipment, with deep experience across charter bus platforms and commercial fleets.

  • AC and heating system diagnostics and repair for buses and commercial vehicles
  • Climate control work for mobile equipment most shops won't touch
  • Charter bus HVAC specialization with real system knowledge
  • On-site and shop-based climate control service

Charter Bus Mechanical Repair

Charter bus repair is a core specialty for NDM. These units demand accurate diagnosis, dependable repair execution, and a shop that understands the urgency of keeping coaches on schedule.

  • Mechanical troubleshooting and repair for charter bus platforms
  • Powertrain, emissions, and electrical diagnosis and repair
  • Support for operators managing active schedules and route commitments
  • Maintenance and repair with commercial operating realities in mind

Over-the-Road Semi Service

NDM supports highway tractors and heavy commercial trucks with a strong focus on uptime, accurate diagnostics, and repair work that solves the real problem the first time.

  • Diagnostics and repair for over-the-road semis
  • Powertrain repair, emissions system work, and advanced electrical troubleshooting
  • Scheduled service and general heavy-duty maintenance
  • Repair planning for commercially critical equipment

Industrial & Mining Equipment Support

Mining and industrial sites rely on diesel-powered support equipment that can't be left down. NDM services pump engines, light plants, and smaller diesel units used in demanding field conditions.

  • Pump engine inspection, repair, and service
  • Light plant and generator support
  • Small diesel engine repair for industrial applications
  • Maintenance help for remote and production-critical equipment
